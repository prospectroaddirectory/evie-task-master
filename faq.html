Questions & answers

A few things people often ask before booking. If there's anything else you're wondering, just pop it in the contact form and my Dad will get back to you.

I've sent my request — what happens now?

My Dad will email you back to confirm I'm free, the price, and our bank details, along with a short summary of exactly what's been agreed. If you're happy, pay the balance by bank transfer. Once that's come through, we'll arrange a quick visit before you go away so you can show me exactly what you'd like done.

What happens if you're poorly or have other plans?

Don't worry — my Mum and Dad will know exactly what's meant to be done, and they'll make sure it all happens as promised. You'll never be left without cover.

What if my pet is unwell while I'm away?

If I notice anything wrong — off their food, low energy, or just not themselves — I'll tell my Mum and Dad straight away and we'll contact you immediately so you can decide what to do. If we can't reach you and it looks urgent, we'll take them to the vet you've told us about. That's why we'll always ask for your vet's details and an emergency contact before you go.

What do you need from me before I go away?

Just a few things so everything runs smoothly: how to get in (a key or door code), what needs doing and when, feeding amounts and where things are kept, your vet's details and an emergency contact for pets, and any alarm codes. We'll go through it all together on the visit before you leave, so nothing gets missed.

Which areas do you cover?

I cover Prospect Road and the nearby streets — anywhere I can easily walk to. If you're not sure whether you're close enough, just ask in the contact form and we'll let you know.
